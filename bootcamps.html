Best Bootcamps OF 2022

Top 5 Best Bootcamps OF 2022







1. CodeSmith
Avg Rating: 4.9/5
OverView: They cover FullStack Javascript courses, including libraries and Frameworks. React, Angular, ETC. They have a part time and full time course, the part time course is 3 days a week. Also The part time course covers 38 weeks of remote programming, while the full time course covers 12 weeks of programming, and is 6 times a week. Choose either remotely, or in-person Located in New York:
Price 2022: 19,000-21,000$
Acceptance Rate: 5%

2. NuCamp
Avg Rating: 4.8/5
OverView: NuCamp is the cheapest on this list, and is very highly rated for such a low price. This way you don't have to worry too much about debt. NuCamp has courses on BackEnd webdevelopment, including: SQL, Python, ETC. They also have Frontend full-stack development, Including: Mobile Development
Price: 400$ - 2000$
Acceptance Rate: 100%

3. App Academy
Avg Rating: 4.7/5
OverView: App Academy has a low acceptance rate just like CodeSmith. However, they have more in person locations. Including: New York, and San Francisco. They also have it where you pay no tuition until you get hired. This may be benefical for some people, specifically people who can't afford to pay that much right now.
Price: 16 weeks- 17,000$
Price: 24 weeks- 20,000$
Acceptance Rate: 5%

4. Hack Reactor
Avg Rating: 4.7/5
OverView: Hack Reactor is one of the hardest bootcamps to get into, but it may be worth getting into. Putting something that is extremely hard to get to, but putting it on your resume may help your chances of getting hired. They offer 3 different options for training, 12 week (FullTime), 36 week (PartTime), and 19 week (FullTime).
Price: 17,000$
Acceptance Rate: 3%

5. FreeCodeCamp
Avg Rating: 4.6/5
OverView: FreeCodeCamp is a great bootcamp and is suprisingly free, they even have some bootcamps that you can follow remotely with other people for free as well. Although it may not be good as a full price bootcamp, it still is very useful. They even have people going and getting 6 figure jobs.
Price: Free
Acceptance Rate: 100%
